How to Register ?

COVID-19 vaccination is free for all Indonesians and long-term residents in Indonesia.

The COVID-19 vaccination programme is being progressively rolled out to all Indonesians people.

All persons aged 12 and above, starting with Indonesia Citizens, can now register for their vaccination below. For individuals below 18 years of age, the parent/guardian should register on behalf of the individual.
